About Us

Hamilton Pools

Hamilton Pools: Your Trusted Partner in Pool Cleaning and Repair. Welcome to Hamilton Pools, where sparkling clean and perfectly maintained pools are our passion! Specializing exclusively in pool cleaning and repair services, we have established ourselves as a leader in the Texas pool care industry. Our commitment to quality service and customer satisfaction is unmatched, making us the go-to choice for pool owners seeking excellence in pool maintenance. At Hamilton Pools, we understand that a swimming pool is more than just a body of water—it's your personal oasis, a place for relaxation and enjoyment. That's why we offer comprehensive pool care services tailored to meet the unique needs of your inground pools. From routine cleaning to complex repairs, our skilled swimming pool service technicians are equipped to handle all aspects of pool maintenance. Our services begin with a thorough assessment of your pool's condition. We check the water chemistry, ensuring the pH levels are balanced, and the chlorine content is optimal for a safe swimming environment. Our technicians are experts in identifying and treating common pool problems like algae growth, mineral buildup, and microorganism presence, ensuring your pool remains a clean and healthy place to swim. Regular vacuuming and skimming are part of our maintenance routine to remove debris and keep your pool's water crystal clear. We also specialize in backwashing and filtering, utilizing high-quality equipment like Pentair pumps and sand filters to maintain the cleanliness and clarity of your pool. Our automated pool cleaners and vacuums work tirelessly to keep your pool free from dirt and debris, making maintenance effortless. Understanding the importance of a well-functioning pool system, we offer comprehensive repair services to address issues with plumbing, heaters, and pumps. Our technicians are proficient in handling repairs, whether it's a minor adjustment or a significant overhaul. We stand by the quality of our work, offering warranties on our services to ensure your peace of mind. Customer service is at the heart of everything we do at Hamilton Pools. Our dedication to customer satisfaction has earned us accolades from reputable organizations like the Better Business Bureau (BBB) and glowing reviews on platforms like Yelp. We believe in building lasting relationships with our customers, offering personalized service that meets your specific pool care needs. In addition to our core services, we provide valuable guidance on pool care and maintenance. Our service flyers, available both online and in print, are a treasure trove of information, offering tips on everything from algae prevention to the proper use of flocculants (floc) for water clarity. At Hamilton Pools, we are committed to using the best products and techniques in the industry. Our use of diatomaceous earth in filtration systems, attention to salinity levels in saltwater pools, and expertise in handling chemicals like calcium and chlorine set us apart. We ensure that every aspect of your pool, from the water to the tile, receives the utmost care and attention. Our team of swimming pool service technicians, supported by knowledgeable customer service representatives, is ready to assist you with all your pool care needs. Whether it's routine maintenance, a complex repair, or advice on pool care, Hamilton Pools is here to ensure that your swimming experience is nothing short of perfect. Choose Hamilton Pools for unparalleled expertise, dedication, and quality in pool cleaning and repair. Let us transform your pool into the pinnacle of cleanliness and relaxation—an oasis in your backyard.
