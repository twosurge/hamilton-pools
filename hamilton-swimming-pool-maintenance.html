    Is it hard to maintain your own pool?

    Maintaining your own pool requires commitment and a learning curve, but it's not overly difficult. Invest in basic equipment, understand chemical balancing, and follow a regular cleaning schedule. Don't hesitate to seek professional help if you encounter major issues.

    Can I shock pool 2 days in a row?

    Go easy on the shock! Avoid back-to-back shocking unless facing severe algae blooms or cloudy water persisting despite initial treatment. Over-shocking can harm your pool surfaces and irritate swimmers. Wait at least 48 hours before considering another shock dose!
